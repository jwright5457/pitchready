Pitch Ready Privacy Policy

Wright Secure LLC · Effective June 14, 2026 · Last updated June 15, 2026

Pitch Ready is built to be private by default. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not include any analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting tools. We, Wright Secure LLC, do not collect, receive, or have access to your information.

Information we collect

None. Pitch Ready does not collect any personal information. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or location. We do not use analytics, advertising identifiers, cookies, tracking, or third-party software development kits that send data to us or to anyone else.

Information you store in the app

You can enter information to run your team, such as pitcher names, jersey numbers, optional player photos, pitch counts, innings pitched, outing dates, team names, and custom pitching rules. All of this is stored only on your device. It is not transmitted anywhere, and we cannot see it.

Network access

Pitch Ready makes no network connections to transmit your data. The app has no account system, no cloud sync, and no backend server. Everything you enter stays on your device. (The app may load only its own bundled files in order to run and work offline; it does not send your information anywhere.)

Children's data

Pitch Ready is intended for use by coaches, parents, and team staff. Rosters may include the names and photos of minors, which you (the user) enter. This information stays on your device. We do not collect this information, and the app does not gather any personal identifiers from any user, including children under 13.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Pitch Ready schedules local notifications on your device (for example, when a pitcher's rest period is up). These are generated on your device and are not sent through us.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives entirely on your device, you can delete it at any time by removing pitchers, teams, or outings in the app. Deleting the app permanently removes all data associated with it.

Third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your information, because we do not have it. Pitch Ready integrates no advertising, analytics, or tracking partners, and relies on no external services to operate.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the revised version at this address.
