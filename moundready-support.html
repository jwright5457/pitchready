Frequently Asked Questions

What devices does Pitch Ready work on?

Pitch Ready runs on iPhone and iPad. Keep your device updated to the latest version of iOS or iPadOS for the best experience.

Can I track a whole roster?

Yes. You can add multiple players and track each one's pitch counts and rest status separately, so you can manage an entire team from one place.

How does Pitch Ready handle pitch-count and rest rules?

You can use built-in pitch-count and rest guidelines, or set your own custom limits to match your league's rules. The app uses whichever you choose to tell you when a player has reached their limit and how long until they're cleared to pitch again.

Where is my data stored?

Your player and pitch data is stored on your device. If you delete the app, that data is removed with it, so be sure you no longer need it before uninstalling.

Do I need an account to use the app?

No. Pitch Ready works without any sign-up or login. You can start tracking players right away.

The app isn't working as expected. What should I try first?

Start with the basics: make sure you're on the latest version of Pitch Ready from the App Store, then fully close and reopen the app. If that doesn't help, restarting your device clears up many issues. If the problem continues, email us with a description of what's happening and your device model and iOS version.

How do I request a feature or report a bug?

We'd love to hear from you. Email jakew@wrightsecure.com with your idea or a description of the bug, including steps to reproduce it if you can.
