Pitch Ready Privacy Policy

Wright Secure LLC · Effective June 14, 2026 · Last updated June 14, 2026

Pitch Ready is built to be private by default. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not include any analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting tools. We, Wright Secure LLC, do not collect, receive, or have access to your information.

Information we collect

None. Pitch Ready does not collect any personal information. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or location. We do not use analytics, advertising identifiers, cookies, tracking, or third-party software development kits that send data to us or to anyone else.

Information you store in the app

You can enter information to run your team, such as pitcher names, jersey numbers, optional player photos, pitch counts, outing dates, team names, and custom pitching rules. By default, all of this is stored only on your device. It is not transmitted anywhere, and we cannot see it.

Optional cloud sync

Pitch Ready includes an optional cloud sync feature that is turned off by default. If you choose to enable it, you provide your own database (a Firebase Realtime Database that you create and control). When sync is on, your roster data (such as names, jersey numbers, photos, and pitch counts) is sent to your own database so it can be shared with people you give the team code to.

  • This data goes to a database you own and configure, not to Wright Secure LLC.
  • We do not receive, store, or have access to data you sync.
  • Anyone you share the database URL and team code with can view and edit that data, so treat them like a password and share them only with people you trust.
  • Data handling on your database is governed by your own database provider (for example, Google Firebase) and your own settings.
If you enable sync, you are responsible for securing your database (for example, by setting access rules) and for the data you place in it.

Children's data

Pitch Ready is intended for use by coaches, parents, and team staff. Rosters may include the names and photos of minors, which you (the user) enter. This information stays on your device, or, if you enable cloud sync, on the database you control. We do not collect this information, and the app does not gather any personal identifiers from any user, including children under 13.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Pitch Ready schedules local notifications on your device (for example, when a pitcher's rest period is up). These are generated on your device and are not sent through us.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you can delete it at any time by removing pitchers, groups, or outings in the app, or by deleting the app. If you used cloud sync, you can remove the synced data directly in your own database, and you can turn sync off in the app at any time.

Third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your information, because we do not have it. The app does not integrate any advertising, analytics, or tracking partners. The only external service involved is the optional cloud database that you set up and control.
