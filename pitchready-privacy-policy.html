Privacy Policy

PitchReady  ·  Effective date: June 12, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Wright Secure LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the PitchReady mobile application ("the app"). We designed PitchReady to work without user accounts and to collect as little information as possible.

Summary

  • PitchReady does not require an account, sign-in, or registration.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or precise location.
  • The content you create in the app is stored on your device.
  • We collect limited, anonymous analytics and crash data to keep the app working well.
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Information We Collect

Information you provide. PitchReady does not ask you to create an account or provide personal information. Content you create or save while using the app is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

Usage and diagnostic data. We use analytics and crash reporting services, which may include Apple's App Store analytics and third-party diagnostic tools, to collect anonymous information about how the app is used and how it performs. This may include:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • General usage events, such as which features are used and how often
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • An anonymous device or installation identifier used to distinguish devices for analytics purposes

This data is not linked to your identity. We have no way to associate it with you personally because the app collects no personal identifiers such as your name or email address.

How We Use Information

We use usage and diagnostic data solely to:

  • Fix bugs, crashes, and performance problems
  • Understand which features are useful so we can improve the app
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the app

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Content you create in PitchReady is stored on your device. If you delete the app, that content is deleted with it.

Anonymous analytics and crash data are transmitted to and stored on secure cloud infrastructure operated by our analytics service providers. This data is retained only as long as needed for the purposes described above, subject to each provider's standard retention periods.

Third-Party Services

PitchReady may use third-party analytics and crash reporting services that process the limited, anonymous data described in this policy on our behalf. These providers are contractually or contractually-equivalently restricted to processing this data only to provide their services to us. We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with any other third parties.

Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell personal information and have not sold personal information at any time. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Delete your data. Because app content lives on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the content within the app or uninstalling the app.
  • Analytics. If you would like anonymous analytics data associated with your device's installation identifier removed, contact us at the address below and we will make a reasonable effort to delete it.
  • Regional rights. Depending on where you live (for example, the EU/EEA, UK, or California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Because PitchReady collects no personal identifiers, we typically hold no information that can be linked to you; however, you may contact us with any privacy request and we will respond as required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

PitchReady is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app requires no account and collects no personal identifiers, no personal information is gathered from any user, including children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we handle, including transmitting analytics data over encrypted connections. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but the minimal, anonymous nature of the data we collect significantly limits risk.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated within the app or on this page. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
